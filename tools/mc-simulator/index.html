Monte Carlo Lattice Simulator

Interactive 2D Monte Carlo lattice simulation of lipid mixing on a triangular (hexagonal) lattice with nearest-neighbor interactions.

Interactive 2D Monte Carlo simulation of lipid mixing on a triangular lattice with periodic boundary conditions and nearest-neighbor pairwise interactions. A binary A/B mixture evolves via Kawasaki exchange dynamics with the Metropolis acceptance criterion. One Monte Carlo cycle corresponds to L² proposed exchanges. See Chaisson, Mehta & Heberle (2026) for details of the model.

Heat Capacity Scan

Sweep ωAB across a range of values and compute the heat capacity C/kB from energy fluctuations at each point. The triangular Ising model has a critical point at ωAB = ln(3)/2 &approx; 0.55 kBT, where the heat capacity diverges. Uses the same lattice size L and composition xA set above.
